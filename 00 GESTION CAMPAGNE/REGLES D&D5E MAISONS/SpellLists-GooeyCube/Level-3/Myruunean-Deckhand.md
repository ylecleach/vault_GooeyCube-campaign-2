Target water vessel that you touch becomes ensorcelled, performing many of the basic sailors' tasks on its own. Ropes tie themselves off or loosen as needed, sails furl and unfurl as needed, the helm becomes easier to handle even during storms, and oars naturally fall into a good rhythm.
